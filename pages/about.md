## Description
This is a demonstration collection of photographs taken by students and faculty affiliated with
Lib350M: Humanities Data Management. The items in this collection are made available
through Creative Commons Licenses. 

## About Lib350M
Lib350M: Humanities Data Management is a required course
for the [Digital Humanities minor](https://dh.uoregon.edu/) at the University of Oregon. 
This course, typically offered in winter term, provides students with essential training in collecting, processing, archiving, and publishing humanities data gathered from galleries, libraries, archives, and museums. Students will learn to curate and manage datasets of images, video, sound, text, and maps.
